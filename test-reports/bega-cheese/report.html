Business Overview

Bega Cheese is one of Australia's largest integrated dairy and food companies and the only ASX-listed Australian-owned company among the "Big Four" dairy processors. The company operates across two segments: Branded (consumer packaged goods — VEGEMITE, Bega Cheese, Dairy Farmers, Dare Iced Coffee, Yoplait, Zooper Dooper) and Bulk (dairy ingredients, nutritional powders, bio-nutrients). Bega sources raw milk directly from dairy farmers, processes it across ~15 manufacturing sites, and distributes through one of Australia's largest cold chain logistics networks serving grocery retail (Coles, Woolworths, Aldi), foodservice, and export markets in Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

Strategic Priorities

  • Manufacturing Consolidation & Cost Reduction: Reducing from 22 to 15 sites, cutting distribution from 100+ to ~75 locations. Target A$80M+ cumulative savings (A$40M achieved FY2025, A$40M projected FY2026). Workforce reducing from ~4,300 to ~3,400 FTE.
  • Branded Segment Growth: Growing higher-margin consumer brands to 7–8% EBITDA growth. Focus on white milk, yoghurt, spreads, and milk-based beverages. Branded EBITDA target ~A$220M.
  • International Expansion: Double-digit growth in international branded business. Building on-ground teams in Singapore, Thailand, and Dubai. Southeast Asia and Middle East as key growth markets.
  • NPD & Innovation Pipeline: "Robust pipeline closely aligned with evolving consumer needs" (CEO Findlay). Recent launches in high-protein wellness products.
  • Balance Sheet Strength for M&A: Net debt reduced to A$126M (FY2025), leverage 0.8x. Consortium bid with FrieslandCampina for ~A$2B Fonterra Oceania acquisition.
Challenges

Pain Points & Challenges

  • Factory closures causing workforce disruption: Strathmerton (VIC) cheese plant closing mid-2026 affecting 300+ jobs, PCA (QLD) also closing. Significant community backlash and political pressure.
  • Complex dual ERP landscape: Running Infor M3 and SAP ECC simultaneously across 200+ legacy application integrations — asset management data likely fragmented across systems.
  • Heavy capex burden: A$60M+ capex in H2 FY2026 plus A$30–35M redundancy costs temporarily increasing leverage to ~1.3–1.4x. Need to ensure maximum ROI on capital deployed.
  • Commodity price headwinds: Rising milk costs and dairy commodity volatility expected over 12–18 months. Need to drive operational efficiency to protect margins.
  • Sustainability gap: Committed to 40% emissions reduction by 2030 but only at 18%. No Scope 3 targets yet. Asset efficiency directly impacts sustainability outcomes.

Engagement Strategy

Recommended Approach

Fit 4 Timing 5 HIGH

Lead with a targeted asset management maturity assessment for one of Bega's sites undergoing transformation (Ridge Street or Laverton). Position it as a low-risk, high-insight engagement ($75K–$100K) that benchmarks their current state against industry best practice. Use the assessment findings to demonstrate the value of advanced analytics across their full 15-site network, converting into a broader $200K–$300K+ analytics implementation programme.

Personalised Talking Points

  1. Transformation timing: "With 22 sites consolidating to 15 and A$60M+ in active capex, now is the critical moment to establish asset management baselines — before the new configurations are locked in."
  2. Benchmarking gap: "You've achieved A$40M in savings this year, but without a structured maturity assessment, how do you know what the remaining 15 sites are truly capable of? Industry benchmarking can identify the next A$20M+ in operational value."
  3. Analytics readiness: "Your IoT investment with Software AG is generating data from farm to factory — a maturity assessment will identify where advanced analytics can convert that data into predictive maintenance and asset optimisation outcomes."
  4. Fonterra integration readiness: "If the Fonterra Oceania acquisition succeeds, you'll need a proven asset management framework to benchmark and integrate an entirely new set of manufacturing operations. Starting now gives you the playbook before Day 1."
