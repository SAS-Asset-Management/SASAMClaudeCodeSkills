### B. Methodology Notes

- **Reporting period:** {{START_DATE}} to {{END_DATE}} ({{DAYS}} days). Previous period: {{PREV_START_DATE}} to {{PREV_END_DATE}}.
- **Index calculation:** (Segment conversion rate / Overall conversion rate) x 100. Values >100 indicate above-average performance; values <100 indicate below-average performance.
- **Lead Quality scoring:** Based on ICP match criteria defined during the discovery interview. Scoring considers job title relevance, seniority level, company fit, and form response quality.
- **Cost per Qualified Lead (CPQL):** Total spend / number of A-grade and B-grade leads only. Provides a more accurate measure of true acquisition cost than headline CPL.
- **Benchmarks:** Australian B2B LinkedIn Ads benchmarks sourced from LinkedIn Marketing Solutions industry reports and aggregated campaign data. Benchmarks are indicative and vary by industry, targeting specificity, and campaign maturity.
- **Currency:** All monetary values reported in AUD unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- **Attribution:** LinkedIn Campaign Manager uses last-touch attribution within its own ecosystem. Default attribution window is 30-day click-through, 7-day view-through. Cross-platform comparison uses GA4 last-click attribution model.
- **Demographic data:** LinkedIn's demographic reporting is based on member profile data, which may be incomplete or outdated. Treat as directional rather than absolute.
- **Creative fatigue thresholds:** Based on LinkedIn Marketing Solutions recommendations and industry best practice for B2B campaigns in the Australian market.

### D. Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **ABM** | Account-Based Marketing -- targeting specific high-value companies rather than broad audiences |
| **CAPI** | Conversions API -- server-side conversion tracking that supplements the browser-based Insight Tag |
| **CBO** | Campaign Budget Optimisation -- LinkedIn's automated budget distribution across ad sets |
| **CPC** | Cost per Click -- total spend divided by number of clicks |
| **CPL** | Cost per Lead -- total spend divided by number of leads generated |
| **CPQL** | Cost per Qualified Lead -- total spend divided by number of A-grade and B-grade leads |
| **CTR** | Click-through Rate -- clicks divided by impressions, expressed as a percentage |
| **ICP** | Ideal Customer Profile -- the definition of the target customer based on firmographic and demographic criteria |
| **Insight Tag** | LinkedIn's JavaScript pixel installed on websites to track conversions and build retargeting audiences |
| **Lead Gen Form** | LinkedIn's native form format that auto-populates fields with member profile data for frictionless lead capture |
| **Matched Audience** | Custom audiences built from website visitors, CRM contacts, company account lists, or lookalike expansion |
| **MQL** | Marketing Qualified Lead -- a lead that meets marketing's qualification criteria and is ready for sales engagement |
| **ROAS** | Return on Ad Spend -- conversion value (or revenue) divided by advertising spend |
| **SQL** | Sales Qualified Lead -- a lead that has been accepted by the sales team as a genuine opportunity |
| **UTM** | Urchin Tracking Module -- URL parameters used to track campaign traffic in web analytics platforms |
| **View-through conversion** | A conversion that occurs after a user sees (but does not click) an ad within the attribution window |
