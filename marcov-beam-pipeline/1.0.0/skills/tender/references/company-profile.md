# marcov Company Profile for Tender Assessment

- ISO 55001 implementation and certification readiness

### 4. Systems & Technology

- Asset data management and cleansing

### 5. Operational Excellence
- Maintenance process improvement
- Work management optimisation

- Contractor management frameworks

## Red Flags (Auto-Decline Indicators)

**Service Type Exclusions** (always decline):
- Construction, capital delivery, upgrades, renewals, installation
- Equipment/goods procurement and supply
- Managed services, operational maintenance contracts
- Labour hire, staff augmentation
- IT infrastructure, software development, platform builds
- Plumbing, electrical, civil works

**Sector Exclusions**:
- Education institutions (TAFEs, universities)
- Healthcare clinical services
- Waste management, cleaning services
- Property/real estate management
- Pure IT/cybersecurity (not OT/asset systems)

**Other Red Flags**:
- Very short timelines with no flexibility (<2 weeks)
- Incumbent has strong relationship and no differentiation opportunity
- Requires certifications we don't hold
- RFI/EOI without clear advisory scope (just fishing for info)
