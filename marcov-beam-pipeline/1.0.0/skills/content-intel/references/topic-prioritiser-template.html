What Is This Report?

This report identifies and prioritises content opportunities discovered from Reddit and community platforms. Topics are scored against SAS-AM's service lines to find the highest-value content gaps — where audience demand is high but existing answers are poor.

How It Works We scan relevant subreddits for recurring questions, pain points, and debates. Each topic is scored across three dimensions: Frequency (how often it's discussed), Relevance (how closely it maps to SAS-AM services), and Content Gap (how poor the existing answers are).
How to Use It Focus on Critical and High priority topics first. Click any row to see the full content brief including our recommended angle, format, and distribution channels. Use the filters to narrow by category or priority tier.
What Happens Next Content briefs feed directly into MBP:linkedin-post for social content, MBP:seo for website optimisation, and MBP:google-ads / MBP:linkedin-ads for paid campaigns. This ensures every piece of content is backed by real audience demand.
