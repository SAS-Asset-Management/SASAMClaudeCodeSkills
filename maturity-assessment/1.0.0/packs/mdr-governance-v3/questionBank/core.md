# Core question bank

Six questions asked in every interview, regardless of role, for cross session
comparability. Each question is Specific, Open, and Traceable: it is framed to
surface a say versus do delta, it maps to at least one maturity subject, and it
carries a pre registered listen for cue the note taker scores against. Ask all
six; do not add to them — role tailored probes come from the cohort files.

## Q1 — The last submission

"Walk me through the most recent MDR submission you were involved in — from the
data leaving its source system to the receiving authority acknowledging it.
What happened on the dates the plan said it would happen?"

- Subjects: 01_scheduleAdherence, 04_reportingFrequency
- Listen for: whether a submission calendar exists in practice, who noticed any
  slippage, and whether a variation was logged or the date quietly moved.

## Q2 — The procedure on the day

"When you or your team last performed a condition assessment, what document was
open — a controlled procedure, a work instruction, or memory? Show me where the
MDR requirement appears in it."

- Subjects: 05_procedureDocumentation, 08_evidenceCapture
- Listen for: a named, versioned procedure versus tacit knowledge; whether the
  MDR standard is cited in the document or only assumed.

## Q3 — The rating decision

"Take a defect you rated recently. How did you decide the rating, and how would
a colleague on another team have rated the same defect?"

- Subjects: 07_ratingScaleCalibration, 06_assessorCompetency
- Listen for: a defined scale with exemplars versus individual judgement, and
  any calibration exercise the interviewee can actually name and date.

## Q4 — The data you do not trust

"Which fields or datasets in the MDR submissions would you personally not rely
on, and why? What happens to a record you suspect is wrong?"

- Subjects: 13_validity, 14_uncertainty, 09_qaProcesses
- Listen for: self disclosed quality gaps (treat as strong evidence), whether a
  QA or exception route exists, and whether caveats are declared downstream.

## Q5 — The decision the data changed

"Tell me about a renewal or maintenance decision in the last year that would
have gone differently without the condition assessment data. Who made it, and
what record shows the linkage?"

- Subjects: 16_investmentLinkage, 19_renewalAlignment, 20_stakeholderConfidence
- Listen for: a traceable decision record versus a narrative claim; whether the
  interviewee can name an asset, a date, and a document.

## Q6 — The blind spot

"Where is the network condition picture weakest — which asset subclasses,
areas, or periods are not being assessed — and who is tracking that gap?"

- Subjects: 21_assetSubclassCoverage, 22_geographicSpread, 25_gapIdentification
- Listen for: a live gap register with owners versus informal awareness, and
  whether the stated gaps match the coverage evidence already on file.
