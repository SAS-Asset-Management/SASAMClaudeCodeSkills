# Cohort probes — field assessor

Performs the physical condition assessments. Probes test lived practice against
the documented method. Frontline observations are attributed at cohort level
only — state this at the start of the session.

## Probes

1. "On your last inspection, what told you what to look at and how to rate it —
   a task card, an app, a colleague, or experience?" — Subjects:
   05_procedureDocumentation, 07_ratingScaleCalibration. Artefact type:
   inspection task card. Typologies: procedural, contradiction.
2. "What evidence do you capture with a rating — photos, measurements,
   position — and what does the protocol require versus what actually gets
   captured on a wet day?" — Subject: 08_evidenceCapture. Artefact type:
   photograph capture protocol. Typologies: evidence, contradiction.
3. "When were you last trained or reauthorised for the asset subclass you
   inspect most, and who holds that record?" — Subject: 06_assessorCompetency.
   Artefact type: training record. Typologies: recency, evidence.
4. "Has anyone ever re inspected or checked your assessments, and how did you
   hear about the result?" — Subject: 09_qaProcesses. Artefact type:
   re inspection record. Typologies: evidence, procedural.
5. "Which assets or areas do you know are overdue for assessment, and how would
   the schedule know that?" — Subjects: 23_assessmentFrequency,
   22_geographicSpread. Artefact type: assessment schedule. Typologies:
   coverage, recency.
