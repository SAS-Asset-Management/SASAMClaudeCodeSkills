# Cohort probes — executive sponsor

Accountable executive for the condition assessment obligation. Probes test
whether governance visibility matches the documented position. Tag each
observed shortfall with its gap typology.

## Probes

1. "The data supply plan commits your organisation to a submission schedule.
   When did the schedule last reach your governance forum, and what did you do
   about any slippage?" — Subjects: 01_scheduleAdherence,
   20_stakeholderConfidence. Artefact type: deliverable tracker. Typologies:
   procedural, evidence.
2. "What proportion of your renewal budget last year can you trace to condition
   assessment findings, and which decision log would show me?" — Subjects:
   16_investmentLinkage, 19_renewalAlignment. Artefact type: decision log or
   business case register. Typologies: evidence, contradiction.
3. "If the receiving authority audited the assessor workforce tomorrow, what
   register would you hand over?" — Subject: 06_assessorCompetency. Artefact
   type: competency matrix. Typologies: evidence, coverage.
4. "Which known coverage gaps have you accepted, and where is that acceptance
   recorded?" — Subjects: 25_gapIdentification, 21_assetSubclassCoverage.
   Artefact type: gap register. Typologies: procedural, recency.
5. "When condition data and the funded programme disagree, who arbitrates and
   where is the variance visible to you?" — Subject: 19_renewalAlignment.
   Artefact type: variance report. Typologies: procedural, contradiction.
