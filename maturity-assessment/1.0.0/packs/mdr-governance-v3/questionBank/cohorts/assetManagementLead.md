# Cohort probes — asset management lead

Owner of the assessment framework, thresholds, and prioritisation. Probes test
the analytical spine between condition records and funded work.

## Probes

1. "Show me the documented prioritisation rules — not the output list, the
   rules — and the version history behind them." — Subject:
   17_prioritisationMethod. Artefact type: prioritisation rule set. Typologies:
   procedural, evidence.
2. "Pick one asset class: what is its intervention threshold, and what is the
   engineering basis recorded for that number?" — Subject:
   18_thresholdDefinition. Artefact type: technical maintenance plan.
   Typologies: evidence, procedural.
3. "How is your internal condition scale reconciled to the MDR condition scale,
   and when was the mapping last exercised across assessors?" — Subject:
   07_ratingScaleCalibration. Artefact type: scale cross walk. Typologies:
   recency, contradiction.
4. "Which recommendations from the last assessment cycle were deferred or
   dropped, and where are the reasons recorded?" — Subject: 19_renewalAlignment.
   Artefact type: reconciliation register. Typologies: evidence, coverage.
5. "What is the assessment interval for your most critical subclass, and how do
   actual inspection dates compare to it over the last two cycles?" — Subjects:
   23_assessmentFrequency, 24_dataCurrency. Artefact type: inspection schedule.
   Typologies: recency, contradiction.
6. "Which subjects in your own view sit below the competent line, and is that
   view written down anywhere?" — Subject: 25_gapIdentification. Artefact type:
   self assessment or improvement plan. Typologies: evidence, procedural.
