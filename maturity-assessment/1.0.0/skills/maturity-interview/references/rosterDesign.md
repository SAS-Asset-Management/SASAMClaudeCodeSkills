# Roster Design — Tiering Heuristics and Session Timebox

## Tiering heuristics

| Tier | Who belongs | Test |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Tier 1 core | Sponsor, accountable executive, process owner, programme manager, one frontline voice per operational stream | Would the scorecard be indefensible without this voice? |
| Tier 2 targeted | Analysts, architects, data managers, interface roles | Does this voice corroborate or contradict a specific Tier 1 claim or delta row? |
| Tier 3 reserve | Everyone else worth naming | Held back; activated only when a session surfaces a signal gap the roster cannot close |

## Budget arithmetic template

State it in the programme document, in this shape:

> Ceiling: 12 sessions (engagement.interviewCeiling). Tier 1: 7 sessions. Tier 2: 4 sessions. Held in reserve: 1 slot for Tier 3 activation. Excluded: the finance lead (no touchpoint on any assessed subject — coverage confirmed via the schedule), the contractor supervisor (evidence reachable through the stream lead's session).

Every exclusion names the reason. An exclusion without a reason is a roster hole, not a decision.

## Default session timebox (45 minutes)

| Minute | Segment | Intent |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0 to 3 | Welcome and framing | Governance review, not an audit; cohort level attribution for frontline voices; observer role acknowledged; consent to capture |
| 3 to 8 | Role and involvement | "Walk me through your role in the process" |
| 8 to 33 | Artefact anchored probes | Core set first, then four to six cohort probes |
| 33 to 40 | Evidence and examples | Specific records, screens, registers — an asset ID, a date, a record to trace |
| 40 to 43 | Pain points and aspirations | "What would make this easier?" |
| 43 to 45 | Close | Summarise threads, confirm follow up evidence requests, confirm quote review right |

Shorter sessions (30 minutes) compress the role segment and the probes. Longer sessions (60 minutes) add a live system walk through. Every session ends with at least one follow up artefact request — no session leaves empty handed.
