# Mode 1 — Editorial Diagram

## When to use

Most posts — explaining a concept, process, or relationship. Default mode when the input doesn't clearly fit Modes 2, 3, or 4.

## Template

Insert the brand frame from `SKILL.md` verbatim at `[FIXED BRAND FRAME]`.

```
Create a clean editorial-style conceptual illustration of [SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT], presented to communicate the idea of [CORE IDEA]. [COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE]. The subject is recognisable but simplified — reduced to clean essential forms, illustrative rather than photographic, in the style of a sophisticated explanatory graphic.

[FIXED BRAND FRAME]

Format: [ASPECT]. Balanced asymmetry, one clear focal point, with [OPEN-SPACE REGION] left as open uncluttered space for adding text later.
```

## Slots

- **SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT** — a recognisable real object reduced to clean essential forms (not abstract shapes).
- **CORE IDEA** — the central argument the illustration should communicate.
- **COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE** — one sentence describing how the subject sits in frame and where the green accent lands.
- **ASPECT** — `a wide 16:9 landscape composition` (default), `a square 1:1 composition`, or `a tall 4:5 portrait composition`.
- **OPEN-SPACE REGION** — e.g. the upper-left quadrant, the right third, the lower band.

## Worked example — condition-based maintenance post

- **SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT:** a stylised industrial diaphragm pump, simplified to clean essential forms.
- **CORE IDEA:** monitoring asset health continuously rather than waiting for failure.
- **COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE:** the pump shown centrally with a few clean indicator points on its key components, one highlighted in green to suggest an early signal being caught.
- **ASPECT:** a wide 16:9 landscape composition.
- **OPEN-SPACE REGION:** the upper-right region.

Assembled prompt:

```
Create a clean editorial-style conceptual illustration of a stylised industrial diaphragm pump, simplified to clean essential forms, presented to communicate the idea of monitoring asset health continuously rather than waiting for failure. The pump shown centrally with a few clean indicator points on its key components, one highlighted in green to suggest an early signal being caught. The subject is recognisable but simplified — reduced to clean essential forms, illustrative rather than photographic, in the style of a sophisticated explanatory graphic.

Visual style: clean, sophisticated, intentional — the look of a high-end consultancy report or quality editorial publication. Flat with crisp clean edges; no outer glow, no halo, no soft luminous bloom, no glassy or frosted texture, no glossy 3D render. Subtle, restrained, confident.

Colour: a deep navy blue (hex #002244) dominates as background and base. A bright lime-green (hex #69BE28) appears ONLY as a precise, deliberate accent — on a single highlighted element, a callout, or a key line. It must never appear as a glow, haze, gradient field, starburst or background light. Support these with cool greys and a soft off-white. Use no other colours.

Lighting: soft, even, calm and assured.

Do not include any text, words, letters, numbers, logos, people or faces. Avoid clutter, neon, rainbow palettes, lens flares, and a generic stock-image look.

Format: a wide 16:9 landscape composition. Balanced asymmetry, one clear focal point, with the upper-right region left as open uncluttered space for adding text later.
```
