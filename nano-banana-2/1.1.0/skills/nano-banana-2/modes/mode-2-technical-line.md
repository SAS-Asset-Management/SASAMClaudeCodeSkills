# Mode 2 — Technical Line

## When to use

Engineering-led posts — FMECA, reliability, asset hierarchies, Maximo data structure, equipment teardowns, failure analysis.

## Template

Insert the brand frame from `SKILL.md` verbatim at `[FIXED BRAND FRAME]`.

```
Create a precise technical line illustration of [SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT], drawn in the style of a clean engineering schematic or isometric technical drawing. [COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE]. Fine, even, deliberate linework in soft off-white on the navy base. The drawing is clean and uncluttered with clear hierarchy — not a busy or noisy blueprint. Callout points or a single key component picked out in the green accent.

[FIXED BRAND FRAME]

Format: [ASPECT]. One clear focal point, with [OPEN-SPACE REGION] left as open uncluttered space for adding text later.
```

## Slots

- **SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT** — the engineering object, simplified to its main assemblies (not every fastener, not photographic detail).
- **COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE** — one sentence describing the schematic arrangement and where the green accent lands.
- **ASPECT** — `a wide 16:9 landscape composition` (default), `a square 1:1 composition`, or `a tall 4:5 portrait composition`.
- **OPEN-SPACE REGION** — e.g. the upper-left quadrant, the right third, the lower band.

## Worked example — FMECA post

- **SIMPLIFIED REAL SUBJECT:** a tram bogie, simplified to its main assemblies.
- **COMPOSITION DIRECTIVE:** a clean exploded isometric view with the major components separated and aligned along a vertical axis, fine connector lines between them, one component picked out in green to indicate a critical failure mode under analysis.
- **ASPECT:** a wide 16:9 landscape composition.
- **OPEN-SPACE REGION:** the upper-right region.

```
Create a precise technical line illustration of a tram bogie, simplified to its main assemblies, drawn in the style of a clean engineering schematic or isometric technical drawing. Colour: a deep navy blue (hex #002244) dominates as background and base. A bright lime-green (hex #69BE28) appears ONLY as a precise, deliberate accent — on a single highlighted element, a callout, or a key line. It must never appear as a glow, haze, gradient field, starburst or background light. Support these with cool greys and a soft off-white. Use no other colours.

Do not include any text, words, letters, numbers, logos, people or faces. Avoid clutter, neon, rainbow palettes, lens flares, and a generic stock-image look.
```
